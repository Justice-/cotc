City of the Condemned

Copyright (C) 2010 Tapio Vierros

Seven Day Roguelike Challenge 2010 Entry.

The game depicts a battle between the armies of Heaven and Hell in a town where innocent people's souls are in danger.

Play as an angel, a divine warrior of God, or as a demon, sinister creation of the Devil.
